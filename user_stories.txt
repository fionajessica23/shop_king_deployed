User Stories for Shop King App:
1. As a user, I want to be able to select products that I want to purchase.

2. As a user, I want to store the product/s in my shopping cart.

3. As a user, I want to be able to increase and decrease the quantities of the product/s in my shopping cart.

4. As a user, I want to be able to remove the product/s from my shopping cart.

5. As a user, I want to be able to clear my shopping cart.

6. As a user, I want to view my total price of my purchase.

7. As a user, I want to be able to type in a discount coupon and apply it to my total price.
